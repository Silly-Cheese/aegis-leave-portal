    Aegis Structure

    Operational authority model

    Owner

    Full control over users, companies, LOAs, audit visibility, and system-wide administration.

    Account Manager

    Can add, edit, and delete people and customer companies, and can add, edit, delete, and end LOAs early across Aegis and customer companies.

    Community Manager

    Can add people within Aegis only and support internal personnel operations without customer-company administration.

    Staff

    Can request LOAs and pull up records for customers based on approved system access.

    Customer Structure

    Customer-side authority model

    Company Owner

    Can manage company people, oversee approvals, and end subordinate LOAs early.

    LOA Manager

    Can manage customer leave workflows and end subordinate staff LOAs early.

    Staff

    Can request LOAs and view their own leave records.
